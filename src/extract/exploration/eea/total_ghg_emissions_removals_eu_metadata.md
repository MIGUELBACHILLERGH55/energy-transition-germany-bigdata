# National emissions reported to the UNFCCC and to the EU under the Governance Regulation, 2025  
**Version:** 3.0  
**Identifier:** eea_t_national-emissions-reported_p_2025_v03_r00  

## Description
National greenhouse gas emission and removal data submitted by countries to the UNFCCC and to the EU under the Governance Regulation.  
The dataset reflects the 2025 GHG inventory submission and includes two-digit country codes.  
Data availability:  
- CSV/DB: 1985–present  
- Excel: 1990–present  

## Pollutants Included
- **In Gg (1000 tonnes):** CO₂, CH₄, N₂O  
- **In CO₂ equivalent:** HFCs, PFCs, SF₆, NF₃ and HFC/PFC mixes  
- **Aggregate:** Total GHG in CO₂ equivalent  

## Temporal Coverage
1985–2023 (annual)

## Themes
- Greenhouse gas inventories (UNFCCC / IPCC CRF)
- Climate change mitigation

## Geographic Coverage
EU Member States and associated countries (e.g., Iceland, Norway, Switzerland)
